Solar Street Lights

Solar street lights are raised light source powered by a solar panel mounted on the lighting structure. The solar panels charge a rechargeable battery which powers the LED lamp during the night. Solar street lights are water resistance and waterproof, they have a low glare and low insect attracting rate. They are dependable and require much less maintenance compared to conventional streets lights. Since external wires are eliminated risk of accident by electric shocks are minimized. Why not contact us to give your streets, home, community a touch of this Ambience.

Solar borehole

Solar borehole operates on power generated using solar PV (Photovoltaic) system. The PV array converts the solar energy into electricity which is used for running the motor pump set. it has low operation costs, low maintenance costs and it is highly dependable. Take advantage of this advanced technology and enjoy steady and healthy water supply year in year out.
